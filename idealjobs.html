Ideal Jobs

The positions of IT Project Manager and Full Stack Software Engineer are similar in the fact they are both closely related to running and working on projects in the IT industry while the Portfolio Management/Asset Management job branches off from the IT industry into investments and money management, with IT knowledge and capabilities to make this job more efficient. Common elements among all three jobs are use of data, possible use of AI. However Project Management and Portfolio Management are both lead management roles Software Engineer is not and is still working among a team of people to complete set projects.
